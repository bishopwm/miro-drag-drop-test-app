Share your virtual business card!

Use this app to quickly fill in your professional details and generate a "business card," using Miro v2 Web SDK's `appCard` capability.

Fill out your primary details, then hit Next to share an optional portfolio. When you're finished, hit Create Card. This will generate an App Card in Miro, based on your profile.
