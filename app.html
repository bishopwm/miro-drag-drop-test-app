Create Business Profile

Use this app to quickly fill in personal/professional details and generate a "business card," using Miro's `appCard` capability.

Once you've filled out these details, hit Next to proceed to the modal details. When you're finished, hit Create Card. This will generate an app card in Miro, based on your profile.
